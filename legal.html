Section 02

Refund & Dispute Policy

Trike Media provides digital services including website design, development, and ongoing maintenance. Because of the nature of creative digital work, our refund eligibility depends on the stage at which a request is made.

Stage Refund Eligibility
Before approval No charge is ever collected before you approve a design direction. If you request a free preview and choose not to proceed, you owe nothing.
After deposit, before delivery The initial deposit is non-refundable. Any amounts paid beyond the deposit will be refunded within 10 business days of a written cancellation request.
After final delivery No refund is available once a completed deliverable has been handed off and accepted. All assets become your property at the time of final payment.
Retainer plans Billing periods already rendered are non-refundable. Future billing may be stopped per the Cancellation Policy below.

To dispute a charge: Contact us at sales@trikemedia.com within 14 days of the invoice date. We will work in good faith to resolve any billing discrepancy before a formal dispute is filed with your card issuer or bank.

Section 03

Cancellation Policy

This policy applies to monthly retainer plans (Steady, Steady+, and Steady Pro). One-time website packages have no recurring billing and do not require a cancellation request.

Term Policy
How to cancel Submit a written request to sales@trikemedia.com at least 7 days before your next billing date.
Active access Service continues through the end of the period you have already paid for.
Early termination fees None. You may cancel at any time without penalty.
Asset handoff All website files, credentials, and project assets will be delivered to you within 5 business days of cancellation.
Section 04

Return Policy

Trike Media does not sell physical goods. All products and services are digital in nature — including websites, design files, copywriting, and SEO work — and a traditional return policy does not apply.

If you are unsatisfied with a deliverable, contact us at sales@trikemedia.com and we will work to resolve it. The revision process specific to your project is outlined in your individual service agreement.

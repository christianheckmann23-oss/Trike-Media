2. Refund & Dispute Policy

Trike Media provides digital services (website design, development, and ongoing maintenance). Because of the nature of digital work, our refund policy is as follows:

  • Before project approval: Our preview-first model means you are never charged before you approve a design direction. If you request a free preview and choose not to move forward, you owe nothing — no questions asked.
  • After project kickoff (deposit paid): If you cancel after paying an initial deposit but before the final deliverable is delivered, you forfeit the deposit. Any additional amounts paid beyond the deposit will be refunded within 10 business days.
  • After final delivery: Once a completed website or deliverable has been handed off and accepted, no refund is available. All creative assets and code become your property at the time of final payment.
  • Retainer plans: Months already rendered are non-refundable. See the Cancellation Policy below for how to stop future billing.

To dispute a charge: Contact us at sales@trikemedia.com within 14 days of the invoice date. We will work with you in good faith to resolve any billing discrepancy before any formal dispute is filed with your card issuer or bank.

3. Cancellation Policy

This policy applies to monthly retainer plans (Starter, Growth, and Authority tiers).

  • How to cancel: Send a written cancellation request to sales@trikemedia.com. Cancellations must be submitted at least 7 days before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the upcoming month.
  • Current billing period: Your service and access remain active through the end of the billing period you have already paid for.
  • No cancellation fees: We do not charge early-termination fees. You may cancel at any time.
  • Asset delivery upon cancellation: Upon cancellation, we will provide you with all website files, credentials, and assets belonging to your project within 5 business days.

One-time website packages do not have a recurring billing component and therefore do not require a cancellation request.

4. Return Policy

Trike Media does not sell physical goods. All products and services are digital in nature (websites, design files, copywriting, SEO, and related digital deliverables). As such, a traditional return policy does not apply.

If you are unsatisfied with a deliverable, please contact us at sales@trikemedia.com and we will work to make it right. Our revision process is outlined in your individual project agreement.

6. Promotional Terms & Conditions

From time to time, Trike Media may offer promotional pricing, discounts, or complimentary services. The following terms apply to all promotions unless otherwise stated in the specific offer:

  • Free preview offer: The complimentary website preview is available to new clients only (one per business). It is provided at Trike Media's discretion and does not constitute a binding contract or guarantee of services. Previews are created based on publicly available information about your business.
  • Discount codes & limited-time pricing: Promotional rates apply only to the initial term specified and do not carry forward to renewals unless explicitly stated. Offers cannot be combined with other promotions unless indicated.
  • Grandfathered pricing: Clients who lock in retainer pricing during a promotional window retain that rate for as long as their subscription remains active and in good standing. Cancellation and re-subscription forfeits the grandfathered rate.
  • Availability: All promotions are subject to availability and may be withdrawn or modified at any time without notice, except for offers already accepted in writing.
  • No cash value: Promotional credits and discounts have no cash value and are non-transferable.

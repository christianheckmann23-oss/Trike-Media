Our Story

We got tired of watching great businesses being overcharged for digital services that were one size fits all.

The learning curve for small business owners to navigate digital services is steep and we saw many local businesses with little to no digital presence. The ones that did have a website were often outdated and not optimized for local search.

We wanted to create a digital agency that was focused on creating a product that was not only functional but also looked great and was easy to use at a price point that was affordable for small businesses.

Trike Media was founded with one belief: that a small business in Elyria, Ohio should be able to compete online just as effectively as a company with a full marketing department. We built our process — preview first, pay later — to prove that belief from day one.
